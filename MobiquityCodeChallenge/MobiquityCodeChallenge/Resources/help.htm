Home Screen

Shows the list of default locations and also locations bookmarked by you. Bookmarked locations will be shown on top and default locations are shown below them. You can delete the bookmarked locations but not the default ones

Adding a location

Click on the plus button on top right.

Map screen will be presented, where pin will be added in current location for the first time or on previously bookmarked location. Drag and drop the pin to the location you want to bookmark.

Click on tick symbol on top right of the screen, after pin is placed in appropriate location to bookmark it. Or click close button on top left to undo the selection and go back to home screen.

You can see that the new location is added to the top of list, on coming back to the home screen.

Deleting a bookmarked location

Each bookmarked location will have a trash icon shown on the right. Clicking on it will show a confirmation dialog, once confirmed, the location will be deleted.

Searching locations

On typing the search text in search bar on top, locations with matching city name will be shown in the list.

Detail Screen

Shows today and week's weather forecast information of the selected location, from home screen. You can select to view today's forecast view or weekwise forecast view. Shows temperature, humidity, wind and other climate related information of the selected location.

Switching between today view and weekwise view

Select an option by tapping on top center control. On tapping 'Today', today's weather forecast will be shown.

On tapping 'Week', weekwise weather forecast will be shown.

Settings Screen

On clicking the second tab of bottom bar, you will find settings screen. On settings screen you can choose the unit for values of temparature, humidity etc on weather forecast screen. Also you can reset all the boomarked locations.

Selecting units

Select an option by tapping Metirc/Imperial view. After selecting, the values displayed on weather forecast info will be in the selected units.

Resetting boomarked locations

Clicking on 'Remove added locations', will show a confirmation dialog, once confirmed, all bookmarked locations will be deleted and will not be shown on home screen anymore.
